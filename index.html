Frequently Asked Questions

How does the billing cycle work?

Your subscription starts the day you sign up and will renew automatically at the end of each billing period. You can change or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes to your subscription will take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoicing options.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.
